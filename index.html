About Me

I am a passionate Hardware Planning and Control Systems Engineer with hands-on experience in WSCAD, Eplan, AutoCAD, COMOS and industrial communication technologies such as Modbus, Profinet, Profibus, and CANopen. My work focuses on control panel design, hardware planning, and system integration for industrial automation projects.

I have contributed to projects across compressor control, plant monitoring, and automation systems, ensuring reliable operation, safety compliance, and efficiency. I am currently seeking new opportunities to apply my expertise and continue growing within advanced automation and electrical design environments.
