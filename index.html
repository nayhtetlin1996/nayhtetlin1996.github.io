About Me

I am a passionate Hardware Planning and Control Systems Engineer with hands-on experience in WSCAD, Eplan, AutoCAD, and COMOS, as well as industrial communication technologies such as Modbus, Profinet, Profibus, and CANopen.

I have contributed to projects at AviComp Controls GmbH in Leipzig, AviComp Singapore, and ST Engineering Urban Solutions, working on compressor control and plant monitoring systems. My foundation in automation engineering was strengthened through hands-on robotics research at the ROAR Lab of SUTD.

My studies have equipped me with a strong theoretical background in Industrial Communication Networks, Logic Control, Non-linear & Adaptive Control, Fuzzy Logic, Neural Networks, Digital & Embedded Systems, Wireless Communication, and Energy Management Systems for Electric & Hybrid Vehicles. I also have practical knowledge in Fault Diagnostics, Model Predictive Control, and Modeling, Simulation, and Optimization in Process Engineering.
